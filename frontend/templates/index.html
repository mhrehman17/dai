About the Decentralized AI System

The Decentralized AI System is a platform that allows for distributed training, collaboration, and deployment of AI models across multiple agents and nodes. Our platform ensures data privacy, model versioning, and offers secure, collaborative AI solutions.

Features of the System

  • Manage Agents: Register, monitor, and manage agents that participate in the decentralized system.
  • Training Control: Orchestrate and control the training of models across different nodes, ensuring decentralized learning.
  • Metrics and Monitoring: Track and visualize training metrics and agent performance using an integrated dashboard.
  • Model Registry: Manage different versions of trained models, allowing for easy tracking and deployment.
  • Security and Privacy: Built-in differential privacy and encryption mechanisms to secure user data.
